✿ so, I finally made this ✿

I've been putting this off for a while now.

Not because I had nothing to say — if anything, I had too much. I just never knew where to put it all. Notes app felt too private. Social media felt too loud. So here we are.

This blog isn't going to be curated or aesthetic or any of that. It's just going to be honest. Some entries will be long, some will just be a sentence or two. Whatever it needs to be.

If you're reading this — welcome. I hope something here makes you feel a little less alone. 🍓
